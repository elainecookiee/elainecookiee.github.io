Digital boarding pass application for frequent travellers


UX/UI Design
Prototyping
Video

Project Overview

This was a project that I worked on during my graduate program at Sheridan. For this project, I collaborated with three team members to come up with an app that would help people access their boarding passes quickly via their mobile device. Each of us participated in a rapid prototype exercise, discussed which features were important or interesting to move forward with and create a video demo.

My Role

UX/UI Designer

Timeline

Feb 2017

Deliverables

Prototyping, Video Animation (After Effects)


Process

Using the 8-6-4-2 Rapid Prototype method, we sketched for 8 minutes, 6 minutes, 4 minutes and then 2 minutes with feedback sessions inbetween. I worked with three group members to design and create a prototype demo video for a mobile application with a boarding pass feature as the main screen.
