Hi there! Welcome to the of my Digital work.

BA Honours Psychology @UW.

During my last year of University, I started meeting people that got me curious about programming, User Interface Design and User Experience Design (UX). I remember going home and being excited about getting a CSS animation to work or the hours spent researching on how to make a good logo.

I am passionate about putting myself out there to try new things -knowing that I makes me uncomfortable. Some activities I took part in were: UW Rock Climbing club, UW Dragon Boat competitive team, Muay Thai, Intramurals Ref, TWSA Events/Promotions Executive, UCLEC Korean Lanugage tutor, Photography club, Soccer, Piano

I have explored and developed my skills in logo development, app icons, posters and vector graphics.

I think the greatest gift is the ability to create, learn, aspire and share.

Enjoy :)!
